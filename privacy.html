Privacy Policy

Swible is designed to offer a focused and reliable app experience while collecting only the information reasonably needed to run, maintain, and improve the service. This page describes the general privacy approach for Swible, a Lonumy product.

Information Swible may process

Swible may process limited information related to app performance, subscription status, feedback and support submissions, and technical or account-related details required to keep the app working properly. This can include analytics events, purchase state, crash diagnostics, device-level technical information, and messages you choose to send through the in-app feedback or support flow.

Why this information is used

The goal is to operate Swible responsibly, understand which features are useful, resolve bugs, respond to support requests, and manage access to premium or subscription-based features. Swible does not collect more information than needed for those general purposes.

Third-party services

Swible may rely on trusted third-party providers to support core app functions. Depending on the feature, this may include services such as RevenueCat for subscription management, Mixpanel for analytics, and Supabase for backend or account-related infrastructure. These services may process relevant data on behalf of Swible to provide their functionality.

Data sales and sharing

Swible does not sell your personal information. Information may be shared only as needed to operate the app, provide support, process subscriptions, comply with legal obligations, or protect the safety and integrity of the service.

Feedback and support

If you need help or want to send feedback, you can use the feedback or support flow inside the Swible app. Any information you submit in that process is used only to review your request, respond when appropriate, and improve the product experience.

Policy updates

This Privacy Policy may be updated from time to time as Swible evolves. Continued use of the app after changes means the updated policy will apply to future use of the service.

Summary: Swible uses limited data to run the app, support subscriptions, understand product usage, and respond to feedback. That data is not sold.
